FAQ

Straight answers before the estimate.

What areas of Rhode Island do you serve?

All of Rhode Island: Providence, Cranston, Warwick, East Greenwich, North Kingstown, Johnston, Coventry, and every town in between. If you're in RI, we can get to you, with no travel fee.

How much does hardwood flooring cost in RI?

Most installs run about $6–$12 per square foot installed, depending on the wood, your subfloor, and the layout. You get a free in-home estimate and a written, itemized scope before work begins.

Do you install bathroom and shower tile?

Yes. Bathroom floors, shower and tub surrounds, niches, and backsplashes, with full waterproofing of every wet area before any tile is set. Porcelain, ceramic, and natural stone.

Do you build and repair decks?

Yes. We handle deck tear-outs, framing repairs, deck boards, stairs, railings, and small rebuilds across Rhode Island. We'll tell you if repair or replacement makes more sense.

Can you refinish my floors instead of replacing them?

Usually, yes. If your hardwood is structurally sound, sanding and refinishing costs far less than replacement and makes old floors look new. We'll tell you honestly which way to go at the estimate.

Are you registered and insured?

Yes. Owner-operated, registered, and insured in Rhode Island. You get a local contractor you can reach directly, with the care of a small family crew.

What happens after I request an estimate?

We contact you to confirm the project and schedule an on-site walkthrough. After seeing the space, we provide a written, itemized estimate so the scope is clear before work begins.
